# Privacy Policy - Expense-Nova

## What Data We Collect
- **SMS Data**: We read SMS messages from your bank to automatically extract transactions.
- **Account Info**: Email and password for authentication.
- **Expense Data**: Amounts, merchants, categories you add manually.

## How We Use Data
- SMS is processed locally on your device, NOT sent to our servers.
- Only the extracted expense details (amount, merchant, category) are sent to our backend.
- We do NOT store, share, or sell your personal SMS data.

## Security
- All communication is encrypted via JWT authentication.
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt.
- Data is stored securely on our servers.

## Your Rights
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
- You can revoke SMS permissions from app settings.
